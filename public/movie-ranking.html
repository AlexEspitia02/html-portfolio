The Best Movies According to Alex

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Stillwater

It's my favorite movie because the portrayal of every character's emotions and features is quite exquisite.

The Soul

It's a Taiwanese movie. The end of the plot has a reverse and every detail is reasonable. The movie lets us think that all events originate from an evil spirit. Actually, it all happened with the medical technology.

The Privilege

Contrary to the movie "The Soul", this movie lets us think that all events originate from medical technology. Actually, it all happened with the evil spirit.
